edit  Hamad Medical Corporation operates the Women s Hospital and Hamad Hospital out of the district     HMC also has the following facilities set up in the district      Human Resources Department  Quality Management Department on Al Rayyan Road  Security Department on Umm Al Obairiyat Street  Hamad International Training Centre on Umm Al Obairiyat Street  The Ministry of Awqaf and Islamic
