       Al Jazeera International Documentary Film Festival  From Wikipedia  the free encyclopedia    Jump to navigation Jump to search Al Jazeera International Documentary Film Festival is an international film festival showcasing documentaries and it is organized by Al Jazeera  It is directed by Abbas Arnaout  The first festival was held on April           Every year the festival has a different theme  The vision of the festival is to make the festival an invitation to introduce worldwide different cultures  foster better relationships through an exchange of experiences and knowledge thus creating a foundation of respect and understanding  Al Jazeera seeks to make the festival become a place where filmmakers from different countries and cultures meet to create a unique platform that celebrates creative talent and encourages a cultural interest in documentary films      It is held at the Doha Sheraton in Doha  Qatar  The festival endeavors to promote worldwide creative talent and in this way  leave behind a unique stamp of originality and professionalism      Winners edit        Io sto con la sposa  On the bride s side      References edit       Archived
