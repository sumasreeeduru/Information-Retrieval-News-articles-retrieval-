       Fred Weir  From Wikipedia  the free encyclopedia    Jump to navigation Jump to search Fred Weir is a Canadian journalist who lives in Moscow and specializes in Russian affairs  He is a Moscow correspondent for the Boston based daily The Christian Science Monitor  and for the monthly Chicago magazine In These Times  He has been a regular contributor from Moscow to The Independent  South China Morning Post    and The Canadian Press     He was also for    years the Moscow correspondent of Hindustan Times an Indian  English language  daily newspaper based in Delhi     Weir is the co author  along with David Michael Kotz  of Revolution from Above  The Demise of the Soviet System  published in       which provides a new interpretation and research for the disintegration of the USSR     Weir studied Russian and Soviet history at the University of Toronto  He lived on a kibbutz in Israel in          travelled extensively around the Middle East  the USSR and Eastern Europe  before
