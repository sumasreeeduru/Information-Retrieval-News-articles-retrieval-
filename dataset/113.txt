Scofield  Bill Frisell  Pat Metheny  Steps Ahead  Charles Lloyd  Toots Thielemans  Stan Getz  Woody Herman  Paul Motian  Peter Erskine  Chet Baker  Michael Brecker Marc Alan Johnson  born October           in Omaha  Nebraska  is an American jazz bass player  composer and band leader  Johnson was born in Nebraska and grew up in Texas  He is married to the Brazilian jazz pianist and singer Eliane Elias      Contents    Career   Discography      As leader     As sideman     See also   References   External links   Career edit  At the age of     Johnson was working professionally with the Fort Worth Symphony  and while at the University of North Texas  he played in the One O Clock Lab Band and was also the principal bassist in the NTSU Symphony  In       Johnson joined  pianist Bill Evans in what would be Evans s last trio  Johnson toured and recorded with Evans until the death of the pianist in          In      he released the tribute album Something For You a tribute to Evans together with his wife  pianist Eliane Elias     His credits since then include albums with Joe Lovano  Michael Brecker  Stan Getz  Bob Brookmeyer  Gary Burton  John Abercrombie  Bill Frisell  Pat Metheny  Eliane Elias  Enrico Pieranunzi  Joey Baron  Philly Joe Jones  Jack DeJohnette  Peter Erskine  Paul Motian and many others  He has also appeared as a leader of his own bands  starting with Bass Desires  a quartet that featured Bill Frisell and John Scofield on electric guitar  with Peter Erskine on drums  which gained international recognition following two ECM recordings in the mid     s  He also recorded two albums of his group Right Brain Patrol for JMT Records  followed by his Sound of Summer Running release on Verve  His ECM release Shades of Jade was featured in Time s October           issue    CDs That Really Swing   as one of the five top jazz releases of the season  It was selected by the Chicago Tribune as one of the    best jazz recordings of      and also received the Danish Music Award for Best Foreign Release in       It features his wife Eliane Elias on piano  Joe Lovano on tenor saxophone  John Scofield on guitar and Joey Baron on drums     As a follow up to Shades of Jade  in       the ECM recording Swept Away was released  This album is a collaboration with pianist composer Eliane Elias  It includes Joe Lovano on tenor saxophone and Joey Baron on drums  The music consists of all original compositions  As a producer  Johnson won two Grammy Awards for co producing Eliane Elias  albums Made in Brazil        and Dance of Time        on Concord Records
