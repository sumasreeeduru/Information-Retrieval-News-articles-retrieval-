view of Aspetar Aspire Zone  also known as Doha Sports City     is a     hectare      km   sporting complex located in Baaya district of Doha  Qatar     Owned by the Aspire Zone Foundation  it was established as an international sports destination in      and in the following year an educational centre for the development of sporting champions  Aspire Academy  was opened     The complex contains several sporting venues  mostly constructed in preparation for the      Asian Games     Aspire Zone is also home to Doha s tallest structure  the Aspire Tower  and is adjacent to Villaggio Mall  the most popular mall in Qatar  The complex is an important feature in the      FIFA World Cup bid submitted by the Qatar Football Association  and was central to the Doha bid for the      Summer Olympics put forward by the Qatar Olympic Committee   Contents    Sporting venues   Aspire Academy   Aspetar   References   External links   Sporting venues edit  Aspire Zone s sporting venues indoor multi purpose sports hall and contains    different playing fields     Aspire Academy edit  Aspire Academy  a sporting academy for youth  is located in the center of Aspire Zone      Aspetar
