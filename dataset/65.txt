Newspaper  Magazine  BroadcastingRelated jobsEditor  Reporter  Writer A columnist is a person who writes for publication in a series  creating an article that usually offers commentary and opinions  Columns appear in newspapers  magazines and other publications  including blogs  They take the form of a short essay by a specific writer who offers a personal point of view  In some instances  a column has been written by a composite or a team  appearing under a pseudonym  or  in effect  a brand name  Some columnists appear on a daily or weekly basis and later reprint the same material in
