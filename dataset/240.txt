Find sources   Folly Bah Thibault    news   newspapers   books   scholar   JSTOR  September       Folly Bah Thibault is a French broadcast journalist of Guinean ancestry  currently working for Al Jazeera English   Career edit  Early Career Thibault began her career in the United States  before moving to Europe and France      France    At France     Thibault served both as anchor and correspondent  She hosted a segment titled The Week in Africa  She also appeared in the Five Years of France    anniversary special  featuring outtakes while presenting the news   Al Jazeera English She now works as an anchor at the main broadcast centre of Al Jazeera English  in Doha in Qatar  where she is a presenter of the flagship programme Newshour  She also hosts studio based interview
